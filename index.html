Our story

A family of groomers who treat your dog like their own

For over ten years, the women of Classi K-9 Clips have been grooming Meridian's dogs from our shop on North Main Street. We are a close knit, family run team, and many of our clients have been coming since before they even had their own dog.

Groomers like Kayla and Becky build real bonds with their regulars. We listen to how you like your dog's cut, we are patient with the nervous ones, and we keep our prices fair so good grooming stays easy to come back to. Gentle grooming for your beloved pets is not just our tagline, it is how we work every day.
